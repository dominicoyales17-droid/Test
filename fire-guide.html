🔥 Fire Safety Guide

Learn how to prepare, respond, and recover safely during fire emergencies.

Overview

Fire emergencies can spread rapidly and threaten lives within minutes. Every student should know how to prevent fires, respond calmly during emergencies, evacuate safely, and follow school procedures.

🚨 RACE Fire Procedure

R

Rescue

Help anyone in immediate danger if it is safe.

A

Alarm

Activate the nearest fire alarm and notify authorities.

C

Confine

Close doors to slow the spread of smoke and flames.

E

Evacuate

Leave immediately using the nearest safe exit.

🔥 Before a Fire

  • Know the nearest emergency exits.
  • Locate fire extinguishers.
  • Avoid overloading electrical outlets.
  • Report damaged electrical equipment.
  • Participate in fire drills seriously.

🚨 During a Fire

  • Stay calm.
  • Activate the nearest fire alarm.
  • Leave immediately using the nearest exit.
  • Never use elevators.
  • Stay low if smoke is present.
  • Proceed directly to the assembly area.

✅ After a Fire

  • Wait for official clearance.
  • Do not re-enter the building.
  • Report missing classmates immediately.
  • Seek medical attention if needed.
