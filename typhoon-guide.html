🌀 Typhoon Safety Guide

Learn how to prepare before, stay safe during, and recover after a typhoon.

Overview

Typhoons can bring strong winds, heavy rainfall, flooding, and power outages. Proper preparation and following school emergency procedures help keep everyone safe.

🌀 Typhoon Safety Stages

📦

Prepare

Monitor weather updates and prepare emergency supplies.

🏫

Stay Indoors

Remain inside classrooms or designated safe areas.

✅

Recover

Wait until authorities declare conditions safe.

🌤 Before a Typhoon

  • Monitor weather advisories.
  • Prepare emergency supplies.
  • Charge electronic devices.
  • Secure loose outdoor objects.
  • Know evacuation routes and assembly areas.

🌧 During a Typhoon

  • Stay indoors.
  • Keep away from windows.
  • Do not go outside during strong winds.
  • Use flashlights instead of candles if possible.
  • Follow school officials' instructions.

☀ After a Typhoon

  • Watch out for fallen electrical wires.
  • Avoid flooded areas.
  • Report damaged facilities.
  • Wait for official announcements before returning to classrooms.
  • Continue monitoring weather updates.
