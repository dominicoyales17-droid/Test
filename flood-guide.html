🌊 Flood Safety Guide

Learn how to prepare before, stay safe during, and recover after flooding.

Overview

Floods can happen suddenly during heavy rains or typhoons. Knowing what to do before, during, and after a flood helps protect lives and reduces risks.

🌊 Flood Warning Levels

🟢

Prepare

Monitor weather updates and prepare emergency supplies.

🟡

Be Ready

Be prepared to evacuate if floodwaters continue rising.

🔴

Evacuate

Move immediately to higher ground when instructed.

🌧 Before a Flood

  • Monitor weather advisories.
  • Prepare an emergency go bag.
  • Know the nearest evacuation center.
  • Charge mobile phones and power banks.
  • Keep important documents in waterproof bags.

🚨 During a Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Avoid walking through floodwaters.
  • Stay away from rivers and drainage canals.
  • Turn off electricity if instructed.
  • Follow evacuation orders.

✅ After a Flood

  • Wait until authorities declare the area safe.
  • Avoid contaminated floodwater.
  • Check for damaged electrical wires.
  • Drink only clean or boiled water.
  • Report hazards to school officials.
