🌎 Earthquake Safety Guide

Learn how to prepare before, stay safe during, and recover after an earthquake.

Overview

Earthquakes happen without warning. Every student should know how to protect themselves, evacuate safely, and remain calm during and after an earthquake.

🦺 Duck, Cover, Hold

🦆

Duck

Get down immediately to prevent being knocked over.

🪑

Cover

Take cover under a sturdy desk or table.

✊

Hold

Hold on until the shaking completely stops.

🌎 Before an Earthquake

  • Know the nearest emergency exits.
  • Locate fire extinguishers.
  • Avoid overloading electrical outlets.
  • Report damaged electrical equipment.
  • Participate in fire drills seriously.

🚨 During a Fire

  • Stay calm.
  • Activate the nearest fire alarm.
  • Leave immediately using the nearest exit.
  • Never use elevators.
  • Stay low if smoke is present.
  • Proceed directly to the assembly area.

✅ After a Fire

  • Wait for official clearance.
  • Do not re-enter the building.
  • Report missing classmates immediately.
  • Seek medical attention if needed.
